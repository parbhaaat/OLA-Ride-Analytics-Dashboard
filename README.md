# 🚖 OLA Ride Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end data analytics solution designed to evaluate the operational performance, revenue streams, and customer satisfaction metrics of OLA ride-sharing services for **July 2024**.

By processing raw ride data, this dashboard extracts actionable insights regarding booking statuses, cancellation trends, payment preferences, and vehicle performance.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** – Interactive 5-page dashboard development  
- **SQL** – Data querying, aggregation, and transformation  
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing  

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Bookings:** 103,024  
- **Total Booking Value:** 35M  
- **Successful Bookings:** 63,967 (**62.09% Success Rate**)  
- **Canceled Bookings:** 28,933  
- **Cancellation Rate:** 28.08%  

---

## 📂 Dashboard Structure & Page Breakdown

### 🔹 1. Overall Performance Summary
**Visuals:**
- KPI Cards (Total Bookings, Booking Value)
- Daily Ride Volume Line Chart
- Booking Status Distribution Pie Chart  

**Insights:**
- ~62% ride success rate  
- Significant losses due to cancellations and driver unavailability  

---

### 🔹 2. Vehicle Type Analysis
**Visuals:**
- Matrix/Table by vehicle type:
  - Prime Sedan  
  - Prime SUV  
  - Prime Plus  
  - Mini  
  - Auto  
  - Bike  
  - E-Bike  

**Insights:**
- Identifies most profitable vehicle segments  
- Tracks:
  - Total Booking Value  
  - Successful Revenue  
  - Distance Metrics  

---

### 🔹 3. Revenue & Payment Methods
**Visuals:**
- Revenue by Payment Method (Bar Chart)  
- Daily Revenue Trends  
- Top 5 Customers Table  

**Insights:**
- **Cash and UPI dominate transactions**  
- Cards contribute comparatively less  

---

### 🔹 4. Cancellation Deep Dive
**Visuals:**
- Cancellation KPI Cards  
- Customer vs Driver Cancellation Pie Charts  

**Insights:**
- **Customer cancellations:**
  - Driver asked to cancel  
  - Driver not moving  
  - Change of plans  

- **Driver cancellations:**
  - Personal/car issues  
  - Customer-related issues  

---

### 🔹 5. Ratings & Satisfaction
**Visuals:**
- Customer → Driver Ratings  
- Driver → Customer Ratings (Matrix by vehicle type)  

**Insights:**
- Consistent ratings across all vehicle types  
- Average rating range: **3.98 – 4.01**

---

## 🚀 How to Use This Dashboard
1. Navigate using the left-side panel across all 5 pages:
   - Overall  
   - Vehicle Type  
   - Revenue  
   - Cancellation  
   - Ratings  

2. Use the **Date Slicer** (top-right) to filter specific days  

3. Hover over visuals to view detailed tooltips and granular insights  

---

## 📈 Key Takeaways
- Moderate success rate (~62%) with room for improvement  
- High cancellation rate (~28%) is a major concern  
- Cash & UPI dominate payment ecosystem  
- Consistent service quality across vehicle categories
